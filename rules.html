Rules & Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria

  • The hackathon is open to participants of all skill levels (beginner, intermediate, expert).
  • Participants must be 18 years or older.
  • Teams can consist of 1 to 4 members.
  • Each participant must register individually. Team formation will be facilitated during the event.

Project Requirements

  • All projects must be built from scratch during the hackathon. Pre-built code, assets, or solutions are not allowed.
  • The project must be related to the hackathon theme, which will be announced during the opening ceremony.
  • Teams must submit their final project and any necessary documentation before the deadline.

Code of Conduct

  • Respect: Participants must show respect to fellow hackers, mentors, judges, and organizers
  • Collaboration: Collaboration is encouraged, but plagiarism or copying code will lead to disqualification.
  • No offensive content: Projects with offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content will be disqualified.
