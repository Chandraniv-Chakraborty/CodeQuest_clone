CodeQuest

2025

CodeQuest 2025 is an exiting 48-hours Hackathon where Developers, Designers, and Innovators from all over the world come together to solve real-world challenges using cutting-edge technology. Wheather you are a seasoned pro or a first time Hacker, CodeQuest provides a platform for creativity , collaborations, and building projects that can make an impact . Teams will work on solving problems in AI , Web development , sustainability , and more , with oppurtunities to network , learn and win amazing prizes.
